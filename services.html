WHAT WE DO

Three pillars.
One outcome.

Every Puzzler engagement starts with one of three pillars — or fits them all together. Capable develops leaders and strengthens operations. Competitive wins the funding. Resilient makes sure it holds.

Capable — Partnered Transformation Competitive — Winning Funding Resilient — Risk & Compliance
PILLAR ONE

Capable.

Partnered Transformation — leadership, workforce, operations, and AI.

Strong organizations are built on strong people and strong systems. Capable is where we partner with your team to develop leaders, strengthen your workforce, document what works, and adopt human-centered AI at a pace you can absorb. Built in from day one. Never bolted on.

What We Deliver

  • Executive and mid-manager leadership coaching
  • Tailored training and capacity-building curricula
  • Team effectiveness, governance, and succession planning
  • Workforce planning, retention, and upskilling strategy
  • Operations documentation and institutional knowledge capture
  • Human-centered AI adoption — pilots, guardrails, and staff fluency
  • Organizational capacity assessments and sprint-ready action plans

Ideal For

Nonprofits navigating growth transitions · Businesses scaling without losing what works · Grantmakers modernizing programs · Federal, state, and local teams building leadership pipelines

PILLAR TWO

Competitive.

Winning Funding Strategies — grants, contracts, and positioning.

Winning takes more than a good idea — it takes positioning, discipline, and inside knowledge of what buyers actually evaluate. Competitive is where we help you pursue and win federal contracts and grants — and turn award into sustained delivery. Grounded in what it takes to win from people who've evaluated both sides.

What We Deliver

  • Federal contracting readiness (SAM.gov, NAICS strategy, capability statements)
  • Grants strategy, prospect research, and proposal development
  • Capture planning and pipeline management
  • Small business certifications (8(a), VOSB/SDVOSB, WOSB, HUBZone, VSBE)
  • Acquisition and procurement modernization
  • Proposal review, color teams, and compliance checks
  • Funding and resourcing strategy for growth-stage businesses

Ideal For

Small businesses entering the federal marketplace · Nonprofits pursuing multi-year federal or foundation grants · Growth-stage businesses positioning for acquisition · Agencies modernizing acquisition pipelines

PILLAR THREE

Resilient.

Risk Correction & Performance Sustainability.

The biggest threats to an organization are often the quietest ones — a weak control, a missed deadline, a compliance gap. Resilient is where we see risk early, correct it before it becomes loss, and build the systems that hold up under audit. Risk correction as a discipline. Not a reaction.

What We Deliver

  • FAR and acquisition compliance assessments
  • Federal grants compliance (2 CFR 200, Uniform Guidance)
  • Internal controls and audit readiness support
  • Operational and enterprise risk assessments
  • Policy and procedure development
  • Post-audit corrective action plans
  • Ongoing advisory retainers and rapid-response risk support

Ideal For

Federal contractors preparing for compliance reviews · Nonprofits managing federal or state grants · Small businesses scaling to meet award requirements · Agencies modernizing risk and compliance posture

The Puzzler Method

The method behind the method.

Most engagements integrate two or three pillars. A nonprofit preparing for a federal grant pursuit might start with Competitive (grants strategy), add Capable (leadership coaching and capacity design), and finish with Resilient (grants compliance readiness).

Work the pieces in the right order, and the whole picture comes together. That's not a metaphor. That's the discipline.
