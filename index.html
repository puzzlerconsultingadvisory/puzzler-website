The Puzzler Method

The method behind the method.

Every engagement starts with a conversation — and runs on a six-step discipline we've refined across two decades of federal, nonprofit, and private-sector operations. Here's how we work.

01

Start With The Edge

Every puzzle starts with the border. We establish the frame first — who you are, where you are, where you're going, and what's in the way. No assumptions.

02

Build The Frame

A rapid three-week diagnostic — capacity gaps, execution gaps, and priorities — built on openness, availability, and real-time observation of how your team actually works.

03

Sort The Pieces

People, processes, resources, and opportunities — sorted into what's working, what's missing, and what's next. The output is a sprint-ready Action Blueprint.

04

Use Visual Cues

Operators don't run on memory. We build visual tools — execution frameworks, performance maps, priority trackers — that your team can see, update, and trust.

05

Take Breaks

Sustained momentum requires intentional pauses. Structured sprint reviews and reset points keep the team oriented, recalibrated, and energized for what's next.

06

Avoid Common Mistakes

Risk correction happens continuously — proactive when we see it coming, reactive when it appears. Spoken or written. Always in the open. Always on the table.
